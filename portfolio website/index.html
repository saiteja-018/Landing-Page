Admissions & Campus FAQs

Find answers to common questions about admissions and campus life

What are the eligibility criteria for B.Tech admission?

You must have completed 12th standard with PCM/PCB. Admissions are based on JEE Mains/Advanced or state entrance exams. A minimum 60% aggregate is preferred.

What are the hostel facilities available?

We provide comfortable on-campus hostels with Wi-Fi, mess facilities, sports amenities, and 24/7 security. Separate hostels for boys and girls with warden support.

How strong is the placement support at Aditya University?

We have 120+ recruitment partners visiting campus every year. Our 95% placement rate includes internships during studies. Average package is 6-8 LPA with top companies like Google, Amazon, TCS, and Infosys.

What clubs and student communities are available?

We have 50+ clubs including coding, robotics, sports, cultural, entrepreneurship, and tech clubs. Students actively participate in hackathons, fests, and competitions throughout the year.

How do I start the admission process?

Fill out the online admission form on our website, upload your 12th marksheet and exam scores, and attend the counseling session. Our admissions team will guide you through every step. You can also contact us via the form below.
